Hi, I’m Davide Baldelli. I am a PhD student at Mila and Polytechnique Montréal, advised by Sarath Chandar and Amal Zouaq.

My research focuses on language model–based agents with an emphasis on memory‑augmented architectures and interpretability, particularly the faithfulness of explanations and internal reasoning.
